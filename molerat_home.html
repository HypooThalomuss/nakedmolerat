What are naked mole rats?

Naked mole rats (Heterocephalus glaber) are a species of rodent that are only found in underground burrows in Eastern Africa. This wesbite was made in order to provide various information about the naked mole rat ranging from their dietary habits, social structure, to predator defence and more.
